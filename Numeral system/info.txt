Your task is to identify minimal possible numeral system of the simple equality X+Y=Z.

Input
Line 1: A string S representing the equality X+Y=Z.

Output
Line 1 : Sought numeral system N.

Constraints
2 ≤ N ≤ 36
1 ≤ Length(X) ≤ 10
1 ≤ Length(Y) ≤ 10
1 ≤ Length(Z) ≤ 10
